  1. Credit Card:

If you choose to pay by credit card we reserve the right to add 3% to your total, Due to credit card processing fees. We will take cash or check with no additional fees.

  1. Late Payment:

Payment or a payment plan within 20 days of work completion is expected. We will add a 20% charge for non payment after 20 days. After 40 days of non payment we will put a lean on the property.

  1. Time and materials jobs:
  1. If your bid has Full Day Time and Materials, Half Day Time and Materials, Hourly Climbing, Fire Mitigation Hourly, Clean up Crew Daily, Clean up Crew Hourly, or Time and Materials Hourly.

For example if we bid you for one full day time and materials we will be there for that day of work.

We do not guarantee that the job will be completed in the estimated time frame. We are always happy to come back for more work; it will be an additional charge.

If you would like to make sure the whole job gets done please let us know and we can do a full bid for all of the work.

In the event of weather or some other reason that we have to leave the job site early, you will get credit for the hours that did not get completed. We will come back at a different date.

  1. Cancellation Policy
  1. We ask that you give us a 3 business day notice if you would like to cancel. If you choose to cancel within 3 business days we will charge you 20 percent of the total job cost. If you choose to cancel the day of we will charge you 40 percent of the total job cost.
